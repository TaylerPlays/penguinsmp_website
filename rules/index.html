PenguinSMP Rules

Please ensure that you follow these guidelines.

1

SMP Participation and Weekly Playtime Requirement

To remain in the SMP you need to play for at least 5 hours per week. If you are not able to do so for a particular reason then you need to inform me or Titus as to why you are not able to play for 5 hours on a certain week. (Note that exceptions can also be made for people who have a busy schedule and are not able to play 5 hours a week.

2

Fair Play and Anti-Cheat Regulations

No cheating is allowed. I acknowledge that this terminology is quite broad but basically this means anything that gives a player unfair advantage over others. For example, using xray or any client modifications using third party cheat client, being given items by a staff member (in that case the staff member and the player both will be punished) etc. I want to make this a fun SMP where everyone enjoys their experience. Players who may attempt to ruin this experience will be punished accordingly.

3

Punishments and Staff Discretion Policy

All punishments are of Staff Discretion, just because something is not stated in the rules, does not mean it is allowed on the SMP. A player can be punished for anything and it is upto the staff members. If you feel that the punishment was unjust then you can appeal the ban.

4

Prohibition of External Server Promotion

Promotion of external Minecraft servers is prohibited within the Penguin SMP. Our community values its unique environment and aims to create an enjoyable experience for all members. Engaging in server advertising disrupts this experience, and as such, we kindly ask all players to refrain from such activities.

5


Promoting Respectful Engagement and Content

To ensure a welcoming and respectful environment on Penguin SMP, we do not permit inappropriate skins, usernames, messages, or builds that could offend others. Violations may result in temporary bans. We aim to nurture a creative, inclusive community that embodies our values of unity and respect.

6

Inclusivity and Respectful Behavior

No racism or disrespect to any community is allowed. I want to make this a open and fun experience for everyone and if there is any targeted harassment towards anyone especially for their race, religion, beliefs etc. then the player will be kicked from the SMP depending on the severity.

If a player fails to adhere to these rules, they may receive a warning initially, followed by the possibility of a subsequent ban.
